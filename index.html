✈️ Quick Travel & Meeting Tips

  • Best US/EU overlap: 8:00–10:00 EST (13:00–15:00 GMT)
  • Asia-Pacific: Late evening US times usually align with early morning APAC times.
  • DST Awareness: Not all countries observe Daylight Saving Time. This tool auto-adjusts using live timezone data.
  • Weekend scheduling: Avoid Friday 15:00+ local time for cross-continental meetings.
