1. No Server-Side Data Collection

Global Time & Meeting Scheduler operates entirely in your browser. We do not use servers, databases, or analytics trackers. No personal data, IP addresses, or usage metrics are collected, stored, or transmitted to third parties.

2. Local Storage Usage

To improve your experience, we use your browser's localStorage to save:

  • Theme preference (Light/Dark mode)
  • Selected cities & URL parameters
  • Notes tab content
  • Affiliate click tracking data (stored locally only)

This data remains strictly on your device. Clearing your browser data will permanently remove it.

3. Third-Party Services

  • QR Code Generation: Uses qrserver.com API. Only the URL you are currently viewing is sent to generate the image. No tracking cookies are involved.
  • Calendar Integrations: Google, Outlook, and Apple/iCal links open in new tabs on their official domains. Their respective privacy policies apply once you leave our site.

4. Children's Privacy

This Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. All data handling occurs locally on the user's device.

5. Data Security

Since no data is transmitted to our servers, there is no risk of server breaches or data leaks. Your notes and preferences are as secure as your device and browser settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact & Feedback

For privacy-related inquiries or to report issues, contact us at mail@mail.com.
